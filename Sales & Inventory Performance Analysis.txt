Sales & Inventory Performance Analysis

1. Weekly Sales Trend per Brand

Based on the Weekly Sales Trend per Brand, SnBrand2 recorded the highest number of units sold. This indicates that SnBrand2 is currently the top-performing brand and a key revenue driver.

2. Stockout Risk Analysis

Referring to the Stockout Days by Brand table, SnBrand2 shows a high number of stockout days (more than 100 days), particularly in the E-commerce channel.
This suggests a significant supply risk despite strong demand.

Implication:
We must prioritize maintaining sufficient stock levels for SnBrand2 to prevent lost sales and revenue leakage.

3. Promotion Uplift Analysis

From the Promotion Uplift Analysis, SnBrand2 generated the highest promotional uplift, approximately 48K units.

This indicates:

The brand responds very well to promotions.

Promotional activities significantly boost demand.

Implication:
Promotions are effective for SnBrand2 and can be leveraged strategically — but stock availability must be secured beforehand to avoid missed opportunities.

4. Price Elasticity Consideration

According to the Elasticity by Brand table, SnBrand2 has demand elasticity greater than 1 (elastic demand).

This means:

Consumers are sensitive to price changes.

Implication:
Price adjustments must be handled carefully. Increasing the price could negatively impact sales volume due to high elasticity.


Strategic Recommendations:
1. Improve Supply Planning
2. Increase production planning for SnBrand2.
3. Reduce stockout days (currently >100 days).
4. Align Promotion with Inventory


Conclusion

SnBrand2 is the strongest-performing brand in terms of sales and promotional responsiveness. However, it faces a high stockout risk, especially in the E-commerce channel.
