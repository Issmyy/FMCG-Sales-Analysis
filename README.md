# FMCG-Sales-Analysis

This dataset is the simulation of daily-level Fast Moving Consumer Goods sales transactions for three consecutive years (2022, 2023, 2024), covering multiple SKUs, Brand, Region, Segments, and Categories. Sales are tracked across Retail, Discount, and E-commerce channels in Central, North, and South Poland, with details on prices, promotions, stock, delivery lag, and pack types. Perfect for practicing demand planner analysis through insight in a business-like scenario.
In this report consists of 9 categories as follows:

1.	Weekly Sales Trend 
    -	This analysis finding weekly sales trend for brand and channel the most sold  
    -	Rank weekly sales trend per Brand
    -	Most contributed Channel on the Peak Week
2.	Promotion Uplift Analysis 
    -	To quantify the sales uplift attribution to promotion, relative to the baseline demand
3.	Price Elasticity Check 
    -	To quantify the sales uplift attribution to promotion, relative to the baseline demand
4.	Lost Sales Detection (Stock = 0)
    -	To identify potential lost sales due to stockouts (Out-of-Stock situations) and quantify their impact on revenue
5.	New SKU 2024 Performance
6.	Channel Mix Shift
    -	To analyze the contribution of each sales channel on an annual basis, based on yearly total sales
7.	Regional Seasonality
    -	Seasonal Pettern per region
    -	Annual market share regional
8.	Pack Type Behaviour 
    - tipe kemasan biasanya mempengaruhi respon terhadap promo, harga, dan penjualan channel
9.	Delivery Performance & Impact on Stock / Sales
